# **App Name**: Saree Shop

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Display a catalog of sarees with images, descriptions, and prices.
- Search and Filter: Enable users to search for sarees by keywords, categories, colors, and other attributes.
- Shopping Cart: Allow users to add sarees to a shopping cart and manage their items.
- Checkout Process: Implement a secure and user-friendly checkout process with shipping and payment options.
- Customer Accounts: Enable users to create accounts to save their favorite sarees and track their order history.
- AI-Powered Saree Recommendation Tool: Use customer profile and purchase history to offer suggestions for items most likely to appeal to the user. The LLM will be a tool to decide when/if to incorporate information such as trending items or social media.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep, saturated maroon (#800000) to evoke richness and tradition.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC) to create a soft and elegant backdrop.
- Accent color: Golden yellow (#FFD700) to highlight important elements and calls to action.
- Body font: 'Alegreya', a serif font lending an elegant, intellectual, contemporary feel; suitable for body text
- Headline font: 'Belleza', a sans-serif with personality and a style aligned to fashion, art, and design; suitable for headlines
- Use high-quality, detailed icons to represent product features and categories.
- Clean and responsive design with a focus on product images and easy navigation.
